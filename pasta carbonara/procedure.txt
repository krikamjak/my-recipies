Bring pasta water to a boil and add salt
Fry guanciale in a pan until golden
Add pasta to pasta water
Turn off heat on pan and seperate guanciale from fat
Mix egg and parmigiano reggiano in a bowl
When pasta is cooked, add pasta to the pan containing fat
Add the egg and cheese mix to the pan with a bit of pasta water
Mix until the sauce has a nice, creamy structure
Add guanciale on top and season with pepper
